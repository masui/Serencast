Serencast

Serencast is a content browsing service where everyone can enjoy “zapping“ to find an entry from a huge hierarchical content database only using two keys (▲ and ▼). Anybody can enjoy viewing contents, and also enjoy broadcasting the list by publishing data on Scrapbox.
